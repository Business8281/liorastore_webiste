Returns

Eligibility

Items must be returned in their original editorial packaging. While we encourage you to test the performance of LIORA cookware, please ensure all components are included in your return shipment.

The Process

1. Access our digital concierge to initiate your request.
2. Print the prepaid carbon-neutral shipping label.
3. Schedule a courier pickup or drop off at any authorized location.

"Conscious craftsmanship means we stand by every curve of our design. If it isn't perfect for your table, it isn't perfect for us."

Refunds

Processing Time

Once your return is received at our atelier, please allow 5-7 business days for our quality team to inspect the pieces. Refunds are issued to the original payment method immediately following approval.

Non-Refundable Items

Final sale items from our 'Archive' collection and customized monograms are ineligible for return unless a structural defect is present upon arrival.

International Orders

We currently offer complimentary returns within North America and the EU. For other regions, shipping costs and duties are non-refundable.

Need guidance?

Our curators are available to discuss care, performance, or return inquiries. We are here to ensure your LIORA experience is seamless.
