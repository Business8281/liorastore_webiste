Guided by Ethics.

At LIORA Editorial, your privacy is not a checkbox; it is a fundamental value. This policy outlines how we curate and safeguard your information when you engage with our digital gallery and purchase our artisanal tools. We maintain a "No-Toxin" approach to data, just as we do for our cookware.

What we gather.

person

Identity Data

Includes your name, email address, and shipping preferences provided during purchase or journal subscription.

payments

Transaction Details

Records of artisanal tools purchased, payment confirmation (processed securely through encrypted gateways), and order history.

Anonymous Curation.

We use technical telemetry to understand how you navigate our editorial pieces. This data is fully anonymized and used only to improve the gallery's rhythm and flow.

Purposeful Intent.

01

Order Fulfillment

Ensuring your consciously crafted tools reach your kitchen with the care they deserve.

02

Editorial Updates

Sharing recipes, artisanal stories, and care guides via our Journal, only when you opt-in.

03

Service Refinement

Analyzing aggregated interactions to refine our digital interface and product availability.

Your Digital Autonomy.

Right to Access

Request a full transcript of the data we hold about your profile at any time.

Right to Erasure

The "Right to be Forgotten." We will purge your identifiable history upon request.

Data Portability

Transfer your information to another curator in a structured, machine-readable format.

Correction

Ensure your details are as accurate as our precision-engineered cookware.

Exercise your rights arrow_forward

Digital Cookies.

We use minimal cookies to maintain your session and shopping bag integrity. Unlike standard e-commerce, we do not allow third-party tracking pixels to follow you across the web.
